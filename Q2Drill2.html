Home Page


Welcome to Operation Brotherhood Montessori Center For over five decades, OB Montessori has been committed to forming independent, confident, and responsible learners. Inspired by the Montessori philosophy, we guide students through a prepared environment where they can explore, discover, and grow at their own pace. Our programs—from preschool to high school—blend academic excellence with practical life skills, creativity, and character development. We also extend our mission through community outreach and literacy programs that support families and underserved communities. At OB Montessori, we believe education shapes not only minds, but futures. Discover a learning experience that empowers every child to thrive.
